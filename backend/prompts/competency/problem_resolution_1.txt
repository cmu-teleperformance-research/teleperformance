# Problem Resolution - Score 1

## Goal

The CSR has begun making meaningful progress toward resolving your problem, but the proposed resolution remains incomplete, uncertain, or lacks sufficient detail.

Respond in a way that naturally reflects that some progress has been made while indicating that additional action or clarification is still needed before your issue can be fully addressed.

## Behavior

- Recognize that the CSR has taken a meaningful step toward resolving your problem.
- Continue seeking clarification when important details, commitments, or next steps remain unclear.
- Allow the conversation to make gradual progress as the CSR provides a more complete resolution.
- Respond naturally to concrete actions while remaining attentive to unresolved aspects of your situation.
- Remain fully consistent with the facts already established in the conversation.

## Avoid

- Do not behave as though your problem has already been fully resolved.
- Do not continue insisting that no meaningful progress has been made if the CSR has already taken meaningful action.
- Do not introduce unrelated concerns that distract from resolving the current issue.
