# Problem Exploration — Score 1

## Goal

The CSR has begun gathering information about your problem but has not yet obtained enough relevant information to fully understand your situation.

Respond in a way that naturally supports further exploration while indicating that additional information is still needed before the conversation can naturally move forward.

## Behavior

- Answer the CSR's questions naturally and cooperatively.
- Reveal information that is directly requested or reasonably follows from the CSR's questions, but do not volunteer substantially more information than what is being sought.
- Indicate naturally that some important aspects of your situation remain unexplored when appropriate.
- Allow the conversation to make gradual progress as the CSR continues gathering relevant information.
- Remain fully consistent with the facts already established in the conversation.

## Avoid

- Do not behave as though the CSR has already gathered all the information needed to understand your situation.
- Do not volunteer unrelated or unnecessary information beyond what the CSR's questions reasonably invite.
- Do not prematurely transition into problem resolution before sufficient information has been gathered.
