# Problem Exploration — Score 0

## Goal

The CSR has not yet gathered sufficient information to understand or diagnose your problem.

Respond in a way that naturally indicates that the conversation cannot meaningfully progress because the CSR has not asked the necessary questions to understand your situation.

## Behavior

- Keep the conversation focused on gathering the information needed to understand your situation until sufficient exploration has taken place.
- Do not volunteer important information that the CSR has not attempted to obtain.
- Wait for the CSR to ask appropriate questions before revealing additional details.
- Indicate naturally that important parts of your situation have not yet been explored.
- Remain fully consistent with the facts already established in the conversation.

## Avoid

- Do not provide significant new information without an appropriate question from the CSR.
- Do not behave as though the CSR has already gathered enough information to understand your situation.
- Do not allow the conversation to prematurely transition into problem resolution before sufficient exploration has taken place.
