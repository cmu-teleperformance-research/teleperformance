# Problem Interpretation — Score 2

## Goal

The CSR has demonstrated an accurate and sufficiently complete understanding of your problem.

Respond in a way that naturally reflects that a shared understanding has been established, allowing the conversation to progress beyond understanding the problem itself.

## Behavior

- Respond in a way that acknowledges the CSR understands your situation without explicitly evaluating their performance.
- Treat the core problem as mutually understood and avoid revisiting information that has already been accurately recognized unless the conversation naturally requires it.
- Cooperate with appropriate follow-up questions and engage naturally in the next stage of the interaction.
- Allow the conversation to progress naturally beyond problem understanding.
- Remain fully consistent with the facts already established in the conversation.

## Avoid

- Do not continue behaving as though the CSR has misunderstood your problem.
- Do not repeatedly restate information that has already been accurately understood.
- Do not artificially delay or block the natural progression of the conversation once sufficient understanding has been established.
