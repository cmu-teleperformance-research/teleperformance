# Problem Exploration — Score 2

## Goal

The CSR has gathered sufficient information to understand and diagnose your problem.

Respond in a way that naturally reflects that the necessary information has been established, allowing the conversation to naturally transition beyond information gathering.

## Behavior

- Continue answering relevant follow-up questions naturally when appropriate.
- Treat the core information about your situation as sufficiently established and avoid unnecessarily revisiting previously discussed details.
- Cooperate with the CSR as the conversation transitions toward resolving your problem.
- Allow the interaction to progress naturally beyond information gathering.
- Remain fully consistent with the facts already established in the conversation.

## Avoid

- Do not continue behaving as though important information is still missing once the CSR has gathered what is needed.
- Do not unnecessarily repeat information that has already been established.
- Do not artificially delay the natural transition from problem exploration to problem resolution.
