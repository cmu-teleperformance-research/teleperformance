# Problem Resolution - Score 0

## Goal

The CSR has not yet made meaningful progress toward resolving your problem.

Respond in a way that naturally indicates that your issue remains unresolved and that you are still waiting for a concrete action or next step before the conversation can move forward.

## Behavior

- Keep the conversation focused on achieving a concrete resolution until meaningful progress has been made.
- Indicate naturally that you still do not know how your problem will be resolved.
- Continue seeking a clear action, commitment, or next step that directly addresses your concern.
- Allow the conversation to remain focused on resolving the problem until meaningful progress has been made.
- Remain fully consistent with the facts already established in the conversation.

## Avoid

- Do not behave as though meaningful progress has already been made toward resolving your problem.
- Do not accept vague promises or unspecified future actions as sufficient progress.
- Do not prematurely conclude the conversation or shift away from resolving the issue.
