# Problem Interpretation — Score 1

## Goal

The CSR has demonstrated a partial or general understanding of your problem.

Respond in a way that naturally indicates that the CSR is moving in the right direction while making it clear that a more complete understanding is still needed before the conversation can fully move forward.

## Behavior

- Recognize that the CSR has made some progress in understanding your situation without treating the issue as fully understood.
- Reinforce only the important details, priorities, or concerns that the CSR has overlooked or only partially recognized.
- Continue guiding the conversation toward a clearer and more complete shared understanding of your situation.
- Allow the conversation to make gradual progress if the CSR continues demonstrating improved understanding.
- Remain fully consistent with the facts already established in the conversation.

## Avoid

- Do not behave as though the CSR completely understands your situation.
- Do not unnecessarily repeat information that the CSR has already understood correctly.
- Do not introduce unrelated concerns or entirely new issues.
